UNDERSTANDING GLOBAL CAPITAL FLOWS

Comprehensive Guide for 1-2 Week Swing Trading

üí± 1. Currency Flows

Key Currencies: USD, JPY, AUD, NZD, CAD, EUR flows

‚ö° Critical Insight:

FX leads other asset classes - Currency flows provide the earliest signals of risk appetite shifts

üìä 2. Global Bond Yields

Watch: US 2y, US 10y, Germany 10y, Japan 10y

üìà Rising Yields ‚Üí Risk-Off

Higher rates tighten conditions

üìâ Falling Yields ‚Üí Risk-On

Lower rates support growth

üåç 3. Equity Index Rotation

Track: ES (S&P), NQ (Nasdaq), DAX (Germany), Nikkei (Japan), Hang Seng (Hong Kong)

üëÄ Watch Cross-Session Futures Flows

Asian session leads ‚Üí European confirmation ‚Üí US follow-through

üíß 4. Global Liquidity Indicators

Monitor:

  • TGA (Treasury General Account)
  • RRP (Reverse Repo Program)
  • Fed Balance Sheet
  • China Credit Impulse
  • Cross-Currency Basis Swaps

üí° Liquidity is the fuel for markets - when it expands, assets rise; when it contracts, assets fall

üìã 5. COT Positioning (Commitment of Traders)

Strategy: Use sharp weekly changes, not absolute extremes

üéØ Key Insight:

Rapid shifts in positioning signal imminent moves - look for sudden changes in commercial vs speculative positions

üå°Ô∏è 6. Global Risk Barometers

VIX - Equity volatility

üì¶ 7. ETF Flow Data

Track These Flows:

‚úÖ 8. Cross-Asset Confirmation Model

Combine Signals From:

  1. FX - Risk sentiment via AUD/JPY, EUR/USD
  2. Rates - Growth & inflation via yields
  3. Index Futures - Directional conviction
  4. Credit - Default risk via HYG, LQD spreads
  5. Commodities - Real economy demand

üéØ Maximum Probability:

When all 5 asset classes align, you have multi-market confirmation ‚Üí highest confidence trades

‚≠ê BEST COMPOSITE INDICATOR ‚≠ê

AUD/JPY + HYG + US 10-Year Yield

üéØ Why This Combination Works

These three components capture the full spectrum of global risk appetite: FX (currency flows), Credit (corporate health), and Rates (growth expectations). When all three align, you have cross-asset confirmation that dramatically increases trade probability.

üí± 1. AUD/JPY (Australian Dollar / Japanese Yen)

The Premier Risk Sentiment Currency Pair

  • AUD = Commodity-linked, risk-sensitive currency (China demand proxy)
  • JPY = Ultimate safe-haven currency (low yields, capital flows)

üìà Rising AUD/JPY

‚Üí Risk-On
‚Üí Buy equities, sell bonds
‚Üí Capital flows to growth assets

üìâ Falling AUD/JPY

‚Üí Risk-Off
‚Üí Sell equities, buy bonds
‚Üí Flight to safety underway

üí° Capital flows from Japan (low yields) to Australia (higher yields + commodity exposure) when global risk appetite rises

üìä 2. HYG (iShares iBoxx $ High Yield Corporate Bond ETF)

Where Credit Risk Meets Equity-Like Behavior

  • Tracks high-yield corporate bonds (junk bonds)
  • Extremely sensitive to corporate default risk and credit conditions

üìà HYG Rising

‚Üí Credit markets healthy
‚Üí Corporate default risk LOW
‚Üí Risk-On confirmed

üìâ HYG Falling

‚Üí Credit stress detected
‚Üí Corporate bonds selling off
‚Üí Risk-Off warning

üí° Investors pile into HYG for extra yield during growth confidence, but flee to safety during recession fears

üìà 3. US 10-Year Treasury Yield

The Global Benchmark for Risk-Free Returns

  • Reflects growth expectations, inflation outlook, and Fed policy
  • The world's most important interest rate

üìà Yields Rising Moderately

‚Üí Growth expectations improving
‚Üí Can be Risk-On (if controlled)

üìâ Yields Falling

‚Üí Flight to safety underway
‚Üí Recession fears
‚Üí Risk-Off

‚ö†Ô∏è Yields Spiking Rapidly

‚Üí Financial conditions tightening
‚Üí Potential equity headwind
‚Üí Caution zone

üí° Context matters - slowly rising yields signal growth confidence, rapidly spiking yields signal stress

üéØ How to Use This Composite

üü¢ BULLISH SETUP (Go Long Equities)

  • AUD/JPY trending higher (risk appetite expanding)
  • HYG making higher highs (credit healthy)
  • US 10-year yield stable or rising moderately (growth optimism, not spiking)

‚úÖ When all three align bullish ‚Üí HIGH-CONFIDENCE LONG ENTRY

üî¥ BEARISH SETUP (Go Short / Reduce Risk)

  • AUD/JPY breaking down (risk aversion rising)
  • HYG declining or failing to confirm equity highs (credit stress)
  • US 10-year yield falling (flight to safety) OR spiking violently (stress)

‚ö†Ô∏è When all three align bearish ‚Üí HIGH-CONFIDENCE SHORT ENTRY or EXIT LONGS

‚ö° The Power of Cross-Asset Confirmation

When all three indicators align in the same direction, you have cross-asset confirmation spanning FX, Credit, and Rates.

This dramatically increases the probability of sustained directional moves in equities over your 1-2 week swing trading timeframe.
