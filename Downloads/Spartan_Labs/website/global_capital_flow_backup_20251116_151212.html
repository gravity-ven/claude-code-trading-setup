Step 1: Buy Country ETF (Easiest)

Expected Return: 20-40% over 12 months
Risk: Moderate (diversified across 50-200 companies)
Allocation: 50% of country allocation

Step 2: Buy Top Companies (Higher Reward)

Example (Australia): BHP, RIO, FMG, CBA, WDS
Expected Return: 30-60% over 12 months
Risk: High (sector concentration)
Allocation: 30% of country allocation (split across 5-7 stocks)

Step 3: Buy Currency (Advanced)

Examples: AUD/USD (FXA), CAD/USD (FXC), INR/USD
Expected Return: 10-25% over 6-12 months
Risk: Low-Moderate (less volatile than stocks)
Allocation: 20% of country allocation
