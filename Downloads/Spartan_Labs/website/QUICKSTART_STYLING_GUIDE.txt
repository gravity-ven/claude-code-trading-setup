  DO'S AND DON'TS
================================================================================

✅ DO:

- Use dark red (#8B0000) for active states
- Use bright green (#00ff88) for positive values

- Use white backgrounds (except printable reports)
- Forget the logo in navigation
- Use low-contrast color combinations
- Create custom reds outside palette
