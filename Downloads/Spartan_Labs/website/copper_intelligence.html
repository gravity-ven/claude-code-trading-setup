üìñ What is COT (Commitment of Traders)?
The CFTC Commitment of Traders (COT) report is published every Friday and shows the positions held by three key groups in the copper futures market:
üè¢ COMMERCIALS (Miners/Users)
Copper miners (Freeport-McMoRan, BHP, Glencore) and industrial users (wire manufacturers, electronics). When they're net SHORT, it's contrarian BULLISH (hedging future production).
üéØ SPECULATORS (Hedge Funds)
Large hedge funds betting on copper price direction. When EXTREMELY LONG, it's BEARISH (market top). When EXTREMELY SHORT or neutral, it's BULLISH (room to run).
üìà SMALL TRADERS (Retail)
Individual traders and small firms. Usually wrong at extremes - when they're extremely bullish, tops are near.
