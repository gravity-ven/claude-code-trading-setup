How to Interpret Crypto Composite Indicator

  • Fear & Greed (Contrarian): Extreme fear (< 20) often marks bottoms. Extreme greed (> 80) often marks tops.
  • MVRV Z-Score: Measures if Bitcoin is overvalued or undervalued relative to its realized value. < 2 = undervalued, > 5 = overvalued.
  • Funding Rates: Negative = too many shorts (bullish). Positive = too many longs (bearish). Extremes (|0.1%|+) signal potential reversals.
  • Bitcoin Dominance: > 55% = money flowing into BTC (risk-off). < 45% = money flowing into altcoins (risk-on).
  • Composite Score: 75+ = Strong Buy Zone | 60-75 = Buy Zone | 40-60 = Neutral | 25-40 = Sell Zone | < 25 = Strong Sell Zone
